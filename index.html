Alexandria Reader

My most recent full-stack project is an open-source language learning app called Alexandria.

Alexandria allows users to upload and read texts of choice, highlight words or phrases, and add translations from built-in dictionaries. A list of vocabulary is also available for review.

Alexandria is built with React, Typescript, NodeJS, Express, and deployed over Netlify and Heroku.
